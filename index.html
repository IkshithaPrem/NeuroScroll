Why We Can't Stop Scrolling

The psychology behind social media addiction

1

Trigger Variable Reward

Unexpected notifications (likes, messages) activate dopamine-seeking behavior. Our brains get hooked on the "maybe" of reward.

2

Action Infinite Scroll

Frictionless interaction (endless content, pull-to-refresh) makes responding effortless. No natural stopping points.

3

Investment Loss Aversion

Time spent curating profiles creates sunk cost fallacy. We fear missing out if we disengage.

4

Repeat Habit Formation

Each cycle strengthens neural pathways. The loop becomes automatic through operant conditioning.
